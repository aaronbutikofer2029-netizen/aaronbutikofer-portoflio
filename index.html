About

Where the stage meets the structure.

I'm a dual-degree student at Northwestern studying Performance Art and Civil Engineering — two disciplines that are more alike than they seem. Both require precision, collaboration, and the ability to build something that holds an audience (or a building) up.

Outside the classroom I write about Northwestern athletics, manage equipment for our teams, sing in Beinen Chorale, coach wrestling, and juggle. I grew up in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, where I was valedictorian of my high school class and earned the rank of Eagle Scout. I care deeply about community, performance, and doing things well.
