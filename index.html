Act II
The Projects
01
DTC · Shriners Children's Hospital · Northwestern
SpicAlign — Car Seat Width System
Partnered with Shriners Children's Hospital to design a solution for children who can't fit safely in standard car seats.
↗
Kids with certain physical conditions can't be positioned safely in standard car seats — the door cart width is wrong for their bodies. We partnered with Shriners Children's Hospital to fix that. SpicAlign is a workflow-integrated guide that shows caregivers the correct seat width parameters and adjusts automatically using telescoping arms, a linear actuator, and foot pedals. From compacted to operational in about 15 seconds — hands-free.
02
DTC · BCA · Northwestern
Bone Conducting Alarm
A alarm that only wakes you up. Using the same technology as a tuning fork.
↗
The Bone conducting alarm was created in my white space section of DTC, meaning we had no project partner. Inspried by a tuning fork we set out to create an alarm that would wake the user up, but not their roomates or other indivuals in the room.
03
Operations · Cedar Rapids Aquatics
Pool Operations Management
Running a public aquatics facility end-to-end — people, schedules, and safety.
↗
Managing a public pool means you're responsible for everything that happens. Scheduling and supervising lifeguards, processing payroll, keeping attendance records, and handling whatever the day throws at you. A few thousand people use this facility every month.
